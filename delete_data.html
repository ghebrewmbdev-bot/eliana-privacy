Delete Your Data — Eliana (ኤልያና)

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Eliana is a Tigrigna language-learning app published by Weegix Technology Solutions LLC. This page explains how to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app, and what is deleted or kept.

In short: Eliana collects almost no data. The app works fully offline and requires no account. The only data ever stored online is anonymous learning progress, and only if you choose to turn on Cloud Sync. You can delete it at any time — directly in the app, or by email.

What data Eliana stores

Eliana does not collect your name, email, phone number, photos, or location. If (and only if) you enable Cloud Sync, the app stores:

  • An anonymous user identifier — a random ID generated by Google Firebase, not linked to your name, email, or identity.
  • Learning progress — which fidel you have practiced, your points, and your daily streak.

If you never turn on Cloud Sync, all of this stays only on your device and is never sent anywhere. Uninstalling the app removes on-device data.

How to delete your data

Option 1 — Inside the app (instant)

Step 1: Open Eliana.
Step 2: Go to Settings.
Step 3: Tap Reset progress and confirm. This immediately clears your learning progress stored on the device.

To also delete any progress stored in the cloud (if you turned on Cloud Sync), use the email request below — this ensures the online copy is removed as well.

Option 2 — Email request

Email: hello@weegix.com
Subject: Eliana Data Deletion Request

Because Eliana accounts are anonymous, we cannot identify you by name or email. To help us locate synced data to delete, include your Eliana user ID if you can find it (Settings → About), or simply let us know and we can guide you. We will process your request within 30 days.

What is deleted and what is kept

Retained backup data contains no personal identifiers and cannot be linked back to you.
